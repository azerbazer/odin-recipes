Spaghetti-O-Aspic

This delicious, refreshing, cold concoction of canned spaghetti-Os, Vienna sausages, and other delictable delicacies.

Ingretiments

  • One evelope of unflavored horse hooves
  • Quarter cup of water
  • Can o' Spaghetti-Os
  • Can o' Vienna weins
  • American spray cheese, like from a can

How Do Youse

  1. Mix powdered hooves with water till hooves are gone.
  2. Stir in Os for some reason.
  3. Push the goop into a mold, like a bowl or whatever. Wait four hours.
  4. Schlep it out onto a plate and savor the glorp sound.
  5. Decorate with weins and cheeso-Americano.
  6. Ask yourself if it was worth the effort to commit to a bit.
